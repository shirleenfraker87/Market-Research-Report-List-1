<p><p>Metal enclosed capacitor banks are categorized into three main market types based on voltage levels: Low Voltage (LV) Capacitor Banks, Medium Voltage (MV) Capacitor Banks, and High Voltage (HV) Capacitor Banks. LV capacitor banks are used for power factor correction in industrial applications, while MV capacitor banks serve utilities and larger industrial settings for voltage support and efficiency. HV capacitor banks are designed for high-end applications in transmission networks, ensuring stability and reliability in power systems at elevated voltages.</p></p>
