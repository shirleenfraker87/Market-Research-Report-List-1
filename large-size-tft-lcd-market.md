<p><p>The large-sized TFT LCD market encompasses various panel technologies, each serving distinct applications. TN (Twisted Nematic) panels offer fast response times but limited viewing angles. IPS (In-Plane Switching) panels provide superior color accuracy and wide viewing angles, making them ideal for professional use. AFFS (Advanced Fringe Field Switching) improves upon IPS with enhanced brightness and contrast. MVA (Multi-Domain Vertical Alignment) panels deliver deep blacks and good viewing angles, balancing performance and cost. Other technologies continue to evolve, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
