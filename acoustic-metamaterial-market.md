<p><p>The acoustic metamaterial market comprises three key types: Resonant Membrane, Periodic Resonators, and Sonic Crystals. </p><p>Resonant Membrane features flexible structures that efficiently absorb sound at specific frequencies. Periodic Resonators consist of arranged units that manipulate sound waves through their geometric design, enhancing or reducing particular frequencies. Sonic Crystals utilize a periodic arrangement of scatterers to create band gaps, allowing for tailored sound propagation and manipulation. Together, these types enable advanced sound control and innovative applications across industries.</p></p>
