<p><strong>The Biogas Liquefaction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cryogenic Technology</li><li>Conventional Upgrading Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biogas liquefaction market comprises two primary types: cryogenic technology and conventional upgrading technology. Cryogenic technology involves cooling biogas to extremely low temperatures, converting it into liquefied biogas (LBG) for efficient storage and transport. This method enhances energy density but requires significant energy input. Conversely, conventional upgrading technology purifies biogas by removing impurities and carbon dioxide, producing biomethane suitable for grid injection or vehicle fuel, focusing on efficiency and lower costs. Both technologies cater to growing demand for renewable energy.</p></p>
