Split Cycle Engines separate intake/compression and combustion/exhaust processes into different cylinders for optimized efficiency. Other technologies include variable compression ratios, lean-burn systems, and advanced combustion techniques. These solutions aim to reduce fuel consumption and emissions while maintaining performance, addressing stricter environmental regulations and consumer demand for sustainability.</p></p>
