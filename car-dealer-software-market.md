<p><p>Car dealer software is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility and accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection, which reduces the need for extensive IT infrastructure. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed locally on the dealer's hardware, providing greater control over data and security, but may require more maintenance and upfront investment. Each type caters to different operational needs and preferences among car dealerships.</p></p>
