<p><ul><li>Single–walled Carbon Nanoparticles</li><li>Multi–walled Carbon Nanoparticles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon nanoparticles market is primarily divided into two types: single-walled carbon nanoparticles (SWCNTs) and multi-walled carbon nanoparticles (MWCNTs). SWCNTs consist of a single graphene tube and exhibit superior electrical and thermal conductivity, making them ideal for applications in electronics and nanotechnology. In contrast, MWCNTs consist of multiple concentric graphene tubes, offering enhanced strength and stability, making them suitable for reinforced materials and composites. Both types are integral in various sectors, including electronics, materials science, and biomedical applications.</p></p>
