<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The OLED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PMOLED</li><li>AMOLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED market is primarily divided into two types: PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED) and AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED). PMOLEDs are simpler and more cost-effective, making them suitable for smaller displays like wearables and simple screens, but they have limitations in size and resolution. AMOLEDs, on the other hand, use an active matrix of OLED pixels, enabling high resolution and larger screens, thus being popular in smartphones and high-end televisions due to their superior image quality and energy efficiency.</p></p>
