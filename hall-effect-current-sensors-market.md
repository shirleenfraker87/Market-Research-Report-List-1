<p><ul><li>Open-Loop Hall Effect Current Sensors</li><li>Closed-Loop Hall Effect Current Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hall Effect current sensor market primarily consists of two types: open-loop and closed-loop sensors. Open-loop Hall Effect current sensors measure the magnetic field generated by current flow, providing a simpler and more cost-effective solution for applications requiring basic current measurement. In contrast, closed-loop Hall Effect sensors utilize feedback mechanisms, offering higher accuracy and stability, making them suitable for demanding applications. Both types serve industries like automotive, industrial automation, and renewable energy, highlighting their significance in modern electrical systems.</p></p>
