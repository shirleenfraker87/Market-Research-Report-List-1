<p><p>Floodlight projectors in the market are primarily categorized into metal-halide and LED types. Metal-halide floodlights offer intense brightness and a broad spectrum of colors, making them suitable for sports venues and large outdoor spaces. However, they consume more energy and have a shorter lifespan. In contrast, LED floodlights are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and provide instant illumination with lower heat emissions. Their adaptability and reduced operating costs make them increasingly popular for both commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
