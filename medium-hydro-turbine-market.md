<p><p>The medium hydro turbine market encompasses various types, including Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and others. Francis turbines are versatile and commonly used for a wide range of heads and flows, making them ideal for medium-sized plants. Kaplan turbines are suited for low-head applications, utilizing adjustable blades for efficiency. Pelton turbines excel in high-head, low-flow situations, converting kinetic energy from fast-moving water. Other turbine types may include crossflow and tubular turbines, each designed for specific operational conditions, enhancing hydroelectric energy production.</p></p>
