<p><p>The graphite electrodes market is categorized into three main types: Regular Power, High Power, and Ultra High Power (UHP) graphite electrodes. Regular Power electrodes are primarily used in lower grade electric arc furnaces, while High Power electrodes offer enhanced performance for medium-grade applications, improving efficiency and electrode lifespan. UHP electrodes are designed for high-performance applications, capable of withstanding extreme conditions in advanced electric arc furnaces, leading to superior conductivity and reduced energy consumption. Each type serves distinct industrial requirements.</p></p>
