<p><p>Bi-drum boilers are classified into horizontal and vertical types based on their orientation. Horizontal boilers feature a horizontal arrangement of drums, allowing for larger steam capacity and efficient heat transfer, often used in power generation and industrial processes. Conversely, vertical boilers have a compact design, making them suitable for limited space applications and quick startup requirements. Both types utilize water and steam separation through their bi-drum configuration, enhancing performance and efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
