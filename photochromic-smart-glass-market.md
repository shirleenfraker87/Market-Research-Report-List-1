<p><strong>The Photochromic Smart Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CdO Glass</li><li>AgX</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photochromic smart glass market is primarily categorized into two types: Cadmium Oxide (CdO) glass and Silver Halides (AgX) glass. CdO glass uses cadmium oxide to enable rapid transition between clear and tinted states when exposed to UV light, offering energy efficiency and UV protection. In contrast, AgX glass incorporates silver halides, providing excellent optical clarity and responsiveness. Both types enhance energy management in buildings and vehicles, allowing for improved comfort and reduced reliance on artificial lighting.</p></p>
