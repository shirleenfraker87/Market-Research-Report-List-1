<p><p>The pozzolan market can be divided into two main types: artificial and natural pozzolans. Artificial pozzolans are materials, often byproducts from industrial processes, such as fly ash, slag, and silica fume, specifically designed to enhance cement properties. Natural pozzolans, sourced from volcanic ash or natural deposits, offer similar benefits and have been used historically in construction. Both types improve concrete durability, reduce permeability, and contribute to sustainability by minimizing the carbon footprint of cement production.</p></p>
