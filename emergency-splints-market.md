<p><p>The emergency splints market is categorized into three main types: rigid, semi-rigid, and malleable emergency splints. Rigid emergency splints provide strong support for fractures and immobilization of injured limbs, typically made from materials like plastic or metal. Semi-rigid splints offer a balance of support and flexibility, often used for various injuries where slight movement is necessary. Malleable emergency splints can be easily shaped to conform to the injured area, providing customized support while ensuring comfort during transport and treatment.</p></p>
