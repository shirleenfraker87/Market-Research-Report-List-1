<p><p>The Dual Energy X-ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) bone densitometer market is segmented into two primary types: axial and peripheral bone densitometers. Axial bone densitometers are typically used in clinical settings for detailed analysis of central skeletal sites like the spine and hip, providing precise assessments of bone mineral density. In contrast, peripheral bone densitometers are portable devices used for quick assessments at sites like the forearm or heel, offering convenience and accessibility for screening and diagnosis in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
