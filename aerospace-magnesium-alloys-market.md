<p><p>The aerospace magnesium alloys market comprises two primary types: casting alloys and wrought alloys. Casting alloys are utilized for complex shapes and components, offering excellent fluidity and adaptability, making them suitable for parts like engine housings. Wrought alloys, on the other hand, are processed through deformation techniques, providing superior strength and ductility, ideal for structural applications such as aircraft frameworks. Both types are crucial in aerospace for their lightweight properties, enhancing fuel efficiency and performance in aviation applications.</p></p>
