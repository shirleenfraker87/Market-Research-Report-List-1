<p><p>The combustible gas sensors market encompasses various technologies used for detecting flammable gases. Catalytic combustion sensors operate by oxidizing gas on a catalyst, producing heat that triggers an alarm. Infrared sensors utilize absorption of infrared light to identify gas concentrations, offering non-contact measurement. Hydrocarbon sensors specifically target gases like methane and propane, while "Others" may include technologies such as thermal conductivity or semiconductor-based sensors. Each type serves distinct applications in safety, industrial processes, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
