<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Coolers</li><li>Plumbed-in Water Coolers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water cooler market comprises primarily two types: bottled water coolers and plumbed-in water coolers. Bottled water coolers use refillable water bottles, making them portable and easy to set up in various locations. They are ideal for environments where a direct water supply is unavailable. On the other hand, plumbed-in water coolers connect directly to the water supply, offering a continuous flow of water with no need for bottle replacements, making them suitable for high-demand settings such as offices and commercial spaces.</p></p>
