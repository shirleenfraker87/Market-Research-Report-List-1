<p><ul><li>Heat Activated Cover Tapes</li><li>Pressure-Sensitive Cover Tapes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-Static Cover Tape market features two main types: heat-activated cover tapes and pressure-sensitive cover tapes. Heat-activated cover tapes bond when subjected to heat, creating a strong adhesion suitable for complex surfaces, while offering superior static protection. In contrast, pressure-sensitive cover tapes adhere upon simple pressure application, making them easy to use and reposition. Both types are essential in electronics packaging, providing reliable protection against static discharge and ensuring the safe transport and storage of sensitive components.</p></p>
