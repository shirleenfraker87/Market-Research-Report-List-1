<p><p>A Reduced Pressure Zone (RPZ) valve is a crucial device used in plumbing and irrigation systems to prevent backflow, ensuring that potable water remains uncontaminated. It operates by maintaining a reduced pressure in the system, creating a physical barrier that prevents contaminated water from flowing back into clean water supplies. RPZ valves are widely utilized in commercial, industrial, and residential settings, particularly where there is a risk of cross-connection with non-potable sources.</p><p>The RPZ Valve Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing regulatory standards regarding water quality and the rising necessity for efficient water management systems.
