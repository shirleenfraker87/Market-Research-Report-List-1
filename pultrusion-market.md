<p><p>Pultrusion is a continuous manufacturing process used to produce composite materials with a constant cross-sectional profile. It involves pulling reinforcing fibers, such as glass or carbon, through a resin bath and then into a heated die, where the resin cures to form a rigid, high-strength composite product. This method is widely used in industries like construction, automotive, aerospace, and electrical due to its ability to create lightweight, durable, and corrosion-resistant materials.</p><p>The Pultrusion Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for lightweight and high-performance materials in various industries, particularly in automotive and construction, where energy efficiency and sustainability are key priorities. The rising adoption of fiber-reinforced composites in renewable energy applications, such as wind turbine blades, is also contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Recent trends in the pultrusion market include advancements in resin formulations, such as the development of bio-based and low-VOC resins, to meet environmental regulations. Additionally, the integration of automation and digital technologies in the pultrusion process is enhancing production efficiency and product quality. Emerging markets in Asia-Pacific and Latin America are expected to offer significant growth opportunities due to rapid industrialization and infrastructure development.</p></p>
