<p><ul><li>Peristaltic Tube Pumps</li><li>Peristaltic Hose Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial peristaltic pumps are categorized into two main types: peristaltic tube pumps and peristaltic hose pumps. Peristaltic tube pumps utilize flexible tubing to create a flow, making them ideal for applications requiring precise metering and handling of delicate fluids. Conversely, peristaltic hose pumps use a reinforced hose, allowing for higher pressures and the ability to handle more viscous and abrasive materials. Both types offer advantages such as minimal contamination, self-priming capabilities, and ease of maintenance, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
