<p><p>The hydraulic fluid reservoir market comprises three main types: mineral oil, synthetic oil, and semi-synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from refining crude oil, offering good lubrication and thermal stability but with limited temperature range. Synthetic oil, engineered from chemical compounds, provides superior performance, especially in extreme conditions, making it ideal for high-demand applications. Semi-synthetic oil blends mineral and synthetic oils, offering a balanced performance with improved properties over mineral oil while being cost-effective, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
