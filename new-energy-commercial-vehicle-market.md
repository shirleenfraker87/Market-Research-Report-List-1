<p><p>The New Energy Commercial Vehicle market comprises three main types: Pure Electric, Plug-in Hybrid, and Fuel Cell vehicles. Pure Electric vehicles operate solely on batteries, offering zero emissions and lower operating costs. Plug-in Hybrids combine electric and internal combustion engines, allowing for extended range and flexibility. Fuel Cell vehicles generate electricity through chemical reactions, producing only water as a byproduct, and provide quick refueling and longer ranges. Each type aims to reduce environmental impact while enhancing transportation efficiency.</p></p>
