<p><p>Voltage supervisor ICs are essential for ensuring proper voltage levels in electronic systems. The market can be categorized into two main types: multiple voltage monitors and single voltage monitors. Multiple voltage monitors are capable of supervising several voltage rails simultaneously, making them ideal for complex applications like telecommunications and automotive systems where multiple voltages must be checked. In contrast, single voltage monitors focus on a singular voltage rail, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness for less demanding applications like consumer electronics.</p></p>
