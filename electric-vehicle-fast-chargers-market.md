<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Fast Chargers market includes two main types: On-board and Off-board Electric Vehicle Chargers (EVC). On-board EVCs are integrated into the vehicle, allowing charging through standard power outlets or slower AC chargers. They are compact and convenient for home use. Off-board EVCs are external, high-power DC chargers installed at public stations, enabling rapid charging by directly supplying power to the battery.
