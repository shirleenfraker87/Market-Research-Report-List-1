<p><p>The phased array devices market is primarily divided into electronic scanning and sector scanning types. Electronic scanning employs multiple antennas to direct radio waves without moving parts, resulting in rapid beam steering and real-time imaging. This method is commonly used in radar and medical ultrasound applications. Sector scanning, on the other hand, involves sweeping a beam across a specific angle or sector, providing detailed information about specific areas. Both methods enhance precision in various industries, including aerospace, defense, and healthcare.</p></p>
