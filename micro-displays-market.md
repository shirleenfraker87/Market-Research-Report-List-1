<p><p>The micro display market encompasses various technologies, including LCD, LCoS, DLP, and OLED, each offering unique advantages. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) is commonly used for its cost-effectiveness and brightness. LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) provides high resolution and contrast, ideal for compact applications. DLP (Digital Light Processing) uses micro mirrors for excellent image quality and speed, often found in projectors. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) boasts vivid colors and flexibility, favored in high-end devices. Additionally, other emerging technologies continue to shape the market.</p></p>
