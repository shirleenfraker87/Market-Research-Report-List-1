<p><strong>The Inspection Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wheel Type</li><li>Crawler-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inspection robots are classified mainly into wheel-type and crawler-type based on their mobility and application. Wheel-type robots are designed for smooth surfaces, offering speed and efficiency for indoor inspections, such as in factories or warehouses. In contrast, crawler-type robots utilize tracks that allow them to navigate rough terrains, obstacles, and uneven surfaces, making them ideal for outdoor inspections, such as pipelines or construction sites. The choice between these types depends on the inspection environment and specific requirements.</p></p>
