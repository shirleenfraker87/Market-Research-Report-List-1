<p><p>The Polyethylene (PE) peelable lidding films market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Heat Sealing Type**: These films bond to containers through heat, creating a strong seal that is tamper-resistant but can be easily peeled back for access.</p><p>2. **Cold Seal Type**: Utilizing a pressure-sensitive adhesive, this type provides an immediate seal at room temperature, ideal for sensitive contents that cannot withstand heat.</p><p>3. **Self-Adhesive Type**: These films feature sticky backs that allow them to adhere directly to packaging without the need for heat or pressure, ensuring fast and convenient application.</p></p>
