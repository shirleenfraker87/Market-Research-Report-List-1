<p><ul><li>Visual LUX Meter</li><li>Photoelectric LUX Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital LUX Meter market is categorized into two main types: Visual LUX Meters and Photoelectric LUX Meters. Visual LUX Meters are designed for qualitative assessments, allowing users to evaluate light levels based on visual readings and comparisons. In contrast, Photoelectric LUX Meters utilize advanced sensors to capture and convert light into electrical signals, providing precise, quantitative measurements of illuminance. Both types serve essential roles in various applications, including industrial, commercial, and environmental monitoring, ensuring optimal lighting conditions.</p></p>
