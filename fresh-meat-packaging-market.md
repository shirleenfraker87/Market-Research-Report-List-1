<p><p>The Fresh Meat Packaging market encompasses various types, including Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP), Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP), and Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP). MAP alters the composition of gases in the packaging to extend shelf life and maintain quality. VSP uses a barrier film that tightly conforms to the meat, providing effective protection and visual appeal. Meanwhile, VTP involves thermoforming processes that create vacuum-sealed packages, enhancing product freshness and reducing oxidation, making them ideal for retail and distribution.</p></p>
