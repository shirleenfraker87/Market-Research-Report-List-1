<p><p>Distributed fiber optic sensors (DFOS) are essential in the power utility market, utilizing single-mode and multimode fiber optics for enhanced monitoring and data acquisition. Single-mode fibers, characterized by a small core diameter, allow for long-distance signal transmission with minimal loss, making them ideal for extensive power networks. Conversely, multimode fibers have a larger core, enabling multiple light pathways, which suit shorter distances and less demanding applications. Both types are crucial for real-time monitoring of infrastructure health and performance.</p></p>
