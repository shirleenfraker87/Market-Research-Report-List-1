<p><p>The Rotary Electrohydraulic Actuator market is categorized into two primary types: Control Type and Switch Type Electrohydraulic Actuators. Control Type actuators provide precise motion control, utilizing feedback systems to regulate position and force, making them suitable for applications requiring fine-tuning. In contrast, Switch Type actuators function as on/off devices, operating in a binary manner, which is ideal for applications needing simple, reliable actuation without complex control mechanisms. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
