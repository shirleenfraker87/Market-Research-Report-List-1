<p><p>Fusion Bonded Epoxy (FBE) coatings are thermoset polymer coatings applied to metal surfaces for corrosion protection. The market is divided into **Internal Surface Coatings** and **External Surface Coatings**. Internal Surface Coatings are used inside pipelines, tanks, and vessels to prevent corrosion and contamination from transported fluids. External Surface Coatings protect exterior surfaces of pipelines, structures, and equipment from environmental factors like moisture, chemicals, and abrasion. Both types enhance durability, extend service life, and ensure operational efficiency in industries like oil & gas, water, and infrastructure.</p></p>
