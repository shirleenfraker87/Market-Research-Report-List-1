<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Motor Cores</li><li>AC Induction Motor Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car traction motor core market primarily includes two key types: permanent magnet motor cores and AC induction motor cores. Permanent magnet motor cores utilize fixed magnets to generate magnetic fields, enhancing efficiency and reducing weight, making them suitable for electric and hybrid vehicles. In contrast, AC induction motor cores operate using electromagnetic induction and are known for their robustness and cost-effectiveness, often employed in hybrid and electric applications. Both types play crucial roles in the performance of electric drivetrains.</p></p>
