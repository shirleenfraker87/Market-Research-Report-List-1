<p><ul><li>Dante Standard</li><li>Livewire Standard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Network Audio Processor market is dominated by two main standards: Dante and Livewire. The Dante standard, developed by Audinate, facilitates the transmission of high-quality audio over IP networks, offering scalability and flexibility for various applications. In contrast, the Livewire standard, created by Telos Alliance, caters specifically to broadcast environments, providing low-latency audio transport and tight integration with radio and television systems. Both standards enhance audio distribution, but target different segments within the professional audio landscape.</p></p>
