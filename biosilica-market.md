<p><p>The biosilica market is categorized primarily into Highly Dispersible Silica and Easy Dispersible Silica. Highly Dispersible Silica is characterized by its fine particle size and high surface area, allowing it to mix seamlessly in various applications, enhancing properties like strength and durability. In contrast, Easy Dispersible Silica offers simpler incorporation into formulations, requiring less energy for mixing.
