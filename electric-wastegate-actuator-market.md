<p><p>The Electric Wastegate Actuator market is divided into two main types: linear and rotary electric wastegate actuators. Linear electric wastegate actuators operate by moving in a straight line to open or close the wastegate valve, offering precise control for turbocharged engines. In contrast, rotary electric wastegate actuators use a rotational motion to achieve similar functionality, often providing a more compact design. Both types enhance engine efficiency and performance by optimizing boost pressure management.</p></p>
