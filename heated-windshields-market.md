<p><p>The heated windshields market comprises two main types: heated wire windshields and heated coated windshields. Heated wire windshields utilize embedded wires to generate warmth, effectively melting ice and snow for improved visibility. In contrast, heated coated windshields feature a conductive thin-film coating that evenly distributes heat across the glass surface. Both types enhance driver safety and comfort, offering solutions for cold weather conditions, thus driving consumer demand in automotive and aftermarket sectors.</p></p>
