<p><p>The magnesium alloys market is categorized into two main types: cast alloys and wrought alloys. Cast alloys are produced by pouring molten magnesium into molds, allowing for intricate shapes and designs, commonly used in automotive and aerospace applications. Wrought alloys, on the other hand, are processed through mechanical shaping, resulting in stronger and more ductile products, suitable for structural components. Both types cater to various industries, offering lightweight solutions that enhance performance and reduce energy consumption.</p></p>
