<p><ul><li>Absorption Grating</li><li>Phase Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Ray Grating market comprises mainly two types: absorption gratings and phase gratings. Absorption gratings utilize the differential absorption of X-rays by various materials to separate wavelengths, enabling high-resolution imaging. In contrast, phase gratings manipulate the phase of X-rays through diffraction effects, enhancing imaging capabilities and sensitivity. Both types are essential in various applications, including medical imaging, materials science, and nanotechnology, as they improve the performance and accuracy of X-ray analysis and imaging techniques.</p></p>
