<p><p>The Anisotropic Magnetoresistive (AMR) Sensor market includes three primary types based on the number of axes measured. A 1-axis magnetic sensor detects magnetic fields along a single direction, making it suitable for basic applications. The 2-axis magnetic sensor measures magnetic fields in two perpendicular directions, allowing for more complex orientation detection. In contrast, a 3-axis magnetic sensor captures magnetic fields in three dimensions, enabling advanced applications such as navigation, motion detection, and robotics, enhancing overall performance and accuracy.</p></p>
