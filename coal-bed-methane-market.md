<p><p>The coal bed methane market involves various techniques for extracting natural gas from coal seams. Horizontal drilling allows for accessing multiple coal seams efficiently, maximizing gas recovery. Hydraulic fracturing enhances permeability by creating fractures in the rock, facilitating gas flow. Proppants, such as sand or ceramics, are used to maintain these fractures open post-fracturing. Together, these methods increase the viability and output of coal bed methane operations, making them crucial for optimizing extraction processes in the energy sector.</p></p>
