<p><p>Office productivity suites can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based suites offer applications hosted online, allowing users to access tools like word processing and spreadsheets from any device with internet connectivity. This model typically includes automatic updates and collaboration features. Conversely, on-premises suites are installed on local machines or servers, providing more control over data and customization but requiring manual updates and maintenance.
