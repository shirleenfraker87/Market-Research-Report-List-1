<p><ul><li>DC Type</li><li>AC Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar surface pump market primarily comprises two types: DC type and AC type. DC type pumps operate on direct current, making them highly efficient for solar energy applications, often used in remote areas where grid power is unavailable. They are generally smaller and suitable for lower power requirements. Conversely, AC type pumps run on alternating current, allowing for higher power applications and longer delivery distances. They are often connected to solar inverters, enabling greater versatility and integration with existing systems.</p></p>
