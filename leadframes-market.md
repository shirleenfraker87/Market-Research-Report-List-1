<p><p>The leadframe market is primarily segmented into two types based on manufacturing processes: stamping and etching. Stamping process leadframes are produced using mechanical presses that shape metal sheets into desired configurations, offering high volume and cost efficiency. In contrast, etching process leadframes involve chemically removing material from metal substrates to create intricate designs, allowing for finer features and enhanced precision. Each method caters to different applications and design requirements in the semiconductor packaging industry, impacting production capabilities and costs.</p></p>
