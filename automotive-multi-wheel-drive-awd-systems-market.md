<p><ul><li>Part Time</li><li>Full Time</li><li>Automatic All-Wheel Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive All-Wheel Drive (AWD) systems enhance vehicle traction and stability across various terrains. Part-Time AWD engages the additional wheels only when needed, offering efficiency during standard driving. Full-Time AWD, or permanent AWD, provides constant power to all wheels, ensuring optimal control in diverse conditions. Automatic All-Wheel Drive dynamically distributes power between front and rear wheels based on real-time traction needs, enhancing safety and performance. Each type caters to different driving requirements, from everyday use to extreme off-road scenarios.</p></p>
