<p><p>The semiconductor IC test handler market comprises various types designed for efficient testing of integrated circuits. Gravity handlers utilize gravity to position the chips for testing, ensuring a simple and effective process. Turret handlers employ a rotating turret mechanism to facilitate multiple test operations, optimizing throughput and flexibility. Pick-and-place handlers use robotic arms to accurately position and handle ICs, allowing for high-speed testing and reduced handling time. Each type addresses specific needs in the semiconductor testing process, enhancing productivity and accuracy.</p></p>
