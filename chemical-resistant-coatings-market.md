<p><p>Chemical-resistant coatings are protective layers applied to surfaces to prevent damage from corrosive substances. The market is segmented into solvent-borne, water-borne, and other types. Solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents as carriers, offering durability and strong adhesion but with higher VOC emissions. Water-borne coatings use water as a base, providing eco-friendliness and lower toxicity, though they may require more drying time. Other types include powder coatings or UV-cured systems, which offer unique benefits like zero VOCs or rapid curing, catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
