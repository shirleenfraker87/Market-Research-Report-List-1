<p><p>Expansive cement is a specialized cement that expands slightly during the hardening process, compensating for shrinkage and preventing cracks. The market is categorized into three types: **K Type**, **M Type**, and **S Type**. **K Type** contains calcium sulfoaluminate, offering controlled expansion for structural applications. **M Type** blends Portland cement with aluminous cement and gypsum, ideal for moderate expansion needs. **S Type** uses high tricalcium aluminate content, providing significant expansion for large-scale projects like bridges and dams, ensuring durability and structural integrity.</p></p>
