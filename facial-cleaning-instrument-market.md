Rotation type instruments feature brush heads that rotate to remove dirt and dead skin, providing a thorough cleanse. Foam type devices often utilize pressurized air to create a rich foam that cleanses the skin while also hydrating it. Each type offers unique benefits catering to diverse skin care needs and preferences.</p></p>
