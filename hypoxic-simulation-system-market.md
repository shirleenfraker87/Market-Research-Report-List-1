<p><ul><li>"Altitude Generator"</li><li>"Altitude Chamber"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hypoxic simulation system market is divided into two main types: altitude generators and altitude chambers. Altitude generators create controlled low-oxygen environments, allowing users to simulate high-altitude conditions for training or research purposes. These devices are often portable and used in various settings. In contrast, altitude chambers are larger, sealed environments that simulate high-altitude conditions for extended periods, offering more comprehensive assessments of physiological responses. Both types are essential for sports training, medical research, and understanding human performance in low-oxygen situations.</p></p>
