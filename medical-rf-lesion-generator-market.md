<p><strong>The Medical RF Lesion Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Radio Frequency (RF)</li><li>Pulsed Radio Frequency (PRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical RF Lesion Generator market comprises two main types: Conventional Radio Frequency (RF) and Pulsed Radio Frequency (PRF). Conventional RF utilizes continuous electrical energy to create lesions in tissues, primarily for pain management and surgical applications, providing immediate results. In contrast, PRF employs short bursts of energy, allowing for a less destructive impact, preserving surrounding tissues while still relieving pain through modulation of nerve activity. Both types are essential in modern medical procedures for targeted tissue treatment.</p></p>
