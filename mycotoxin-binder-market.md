<p><p>The mycotoxin binder market includes several types, primarily raw clay, zeolites, aluminosilicates, and polysaccharides. Aluminosilicates, composed of alumina and silica, offer strong binding properties. Polysaccharides, derived from natural sources, provide binding through their structural characteristics. Each type serves to mitigate the toxic effects of mycotoxins in agricultural products, ensuring safer consumption and improving overall food safety.</p></p>
