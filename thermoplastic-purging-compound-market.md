<p><p>The Thermoplastic Purging Compound market is segmented into three main types: Mechanical Purging, Chemical/Foaming Purging, and Liquid Purging. Mechanical Purging involves physical methods to clear residue from machinery by using solid materials, enhancing cleaning efficiency. Chemical/Foaming Purging utilizes chemical agents that generate foam to dissolve and remove contaminants. Liquid Purging employs specially formulated liquids to flush out residual thermoplastic materials, ensuring thorough cleaning. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to different processing needs within the thermoplastics industry.</p></p>
