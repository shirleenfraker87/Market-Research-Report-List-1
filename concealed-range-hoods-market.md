<p><strong>The Concealed Range Hoods Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow</li><li>Centrifugal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concealed range hoods market features two primary types: axial flow and centrifugal hoods. Axial flow hoods utilize fans to move air parallel to the axis, making them effective for low-pressure applications and quieter operation. Centrifugal hoods, on the other hand, use fans that draw air in and expel it at right angles, creating higher pressure for improved exhaust efficiency. Both types cater to varying kitchen environments, influenced by factors like design preferences, airflow requirements, and installation options.</p></p>
