<p><p>The Cleanroom Air Filter Market primarily encompasses HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters and ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters. HEPA filters capture at least 99.97% of airborne particles 0.3 microns in size, making them suitable for applications requiring high levels of air cleanliness, such as pharmaceuticals and electronics. ULPA filters, with even higher efficiency, capture 99.999% of particles down to 0.1 microns, offering enhanced filtration for more stringent cleanroom standards. Both filter types are essential in maintaining controlled environments.</p></p>
