<p><p>The plastic bottle crusher market is categorized into two main types: single-axis and multi-axis crushers. Single-axis plastic bottle crushers utilize a single rotating shaft with blades to efficiently crush bottles, making them ideal for low to moderate volume processing. In contrast, multi-axis plastic bottle crushers feature multiple rotating shafts, allowing for higher throughput and the ability to handle larger bottles and thicker materials. Both types play a crucial role in recycling and waste management by reducing the volume of plastic waste.</p></p>
