<p><p>The wood primers market comprises three main types: oil-based, water-based, and shellac-based primers. Oil-based primers offer excellent adhesion and durability, making them ideal for exterior applications and rough surfaces. Water-based primers are eco-friendly, quick-drying, and easily cleanable, suitable for interior projects. Shellac-based primers provide strong stain-blocking capabilities and fast drying times, effectively sealing knots and preventing bleed-through. Each type has unique properties that cater to diverse needs in wood preparation and finishing.</p></p>
