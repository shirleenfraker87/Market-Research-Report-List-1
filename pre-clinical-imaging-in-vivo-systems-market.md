<p><p>The pre-clinical imaging (in-vivo) systems market includes various technologies for visualizing biological processes in living organisms. Optical imaging systems provide high-resolution images through fluorescence and bioluminescence. Nuclear imaging systems, like PET and SPECT, evaluate metabolic activity using radioactive tracers. Micro-MRI offers detailed anatomical images at a micro-scale, while micro-ultrasound enhances spatial resolution for small animals. Photoacoustic imaging combines optical and ultrasound techniques for functional imaging, whereas magnetic particle imaging visualizes biological dynamics using magnetic nanoparticles. Other emerging technologies also contribute to this diverse market.</p></p>
